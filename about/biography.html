BIOGRAPHY

Jia Xu is a professor in the School of Computer Science & Technology, School of Software at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, P.R. China. He received his B.S.and M.S. in Computer Science from Yangzhou University, P.R. China in 2003 and 2006, and Ph.D. in Computer Science from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, P.R. China in 2010. He has visited the Colorado School of Mines(CSM) as a visiting scholar. His research interests include wireless networking, algorithm game theory. He is a member of ACM and IEEE.
